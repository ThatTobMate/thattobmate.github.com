About Me

Oct 16, 2015

Welcome to CodeCrunch.io, this is my personal blogging site where I will write guides, fixes and any issues I have found as a developer.
I started web development in June 2014 and I have learnt SO much in that time and I found reading blog posts, articles and stack overflow a huge part of my learning process.
Now that I am working fulltime at a digital agency in London I thought it would be a good time for me to write some posts about what problems I come across in my day to day life as a developer (there are many!) and hopefully developers new and old can learn something from my posts. I primarily work with Ruby on Rails and AngularJs, so the majority of my posts will focus around these, but I will be teaching myself Node fairly soon.

I hope you enjoy this blog and any feedback would be great. If you want to ask me any questions then you can follow me on Twitter: @ThatTobMate
