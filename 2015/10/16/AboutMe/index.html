Welcome To CodeCrunch.io

Oct 16, 2015

Welcome to CodeCrunch.io, this is my personal blogging website where I will write guides, fixes
and any issues that I have found as a developer.
I started web development in June 2014 and I have learnt so much in that time and I found
reading blog posts, articles and stack overflow to be a huge part of my learning process.
Now that I am working fulltime at a digital agency in London I thought it would
be a good time for me to write some posts about the problems that I come across in my day to day
life as a developer (there are many!) and hopefully developers new and old can learn something
from my posts. I primarily work with Ruby on Rails and AngularJs, so the majority of my posts
will focus around these, but I will be teaching myself Node fairly soon.

I hope you enjoy this blog and any feedback would be great. If you want to ask me any questions
then just leave comments below or you can follow me on Twitter: @ThatTobMate
